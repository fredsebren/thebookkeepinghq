Frequently asked questions.

We use a flat monthly rate. We'll talk about your specific business needs and figure out how we can best serve you.

We specialize in QuickBooks and are QuickBooks Online Certified ProAdvisors. We work remotely or can set up in-person meetings to help you better understand your bookkeeping. We use secure cloud-based software for easy document uploads and transfers.

We analyze and record all of your business transactions through QuickBooks to compile monthly financial statements such as a Profit & Loss (P&L) and Balance Sheet.

We provide excellent customer service and are here to serve you. We can absolutely meet more often to fit your specific business needs.

We'll need your bank and/or credit card statements every month. We can set statements to sync automatically with QuickBooks so you can focus on your business while we handle the books — or you can send documents yourself. We use secure cloud-based software and email for delivery.

We deliver your financial reports by the 10th of every month so you can better plan for your business.
