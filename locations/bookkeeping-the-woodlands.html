A neighbor, not a call center

The Woodlands runs on small businesses. Dana keeps their books honest.

From the shops around Market Street to the contractors, clinics, and consultancies tucked through the villages, this is a town built by owner-operators.

Those owners don't need a faceless national franchise routing them to whoever's available. They need one experienced professional who knows their file, picks up the phone, and treats their business like it matters — because in a boutique practice, it does. Dana personally handles every client — in The Woodlands or across the country — with no junior team and no hand-off. Being local just means you also get the option of meeting in person.

And because everything runs in QuickBooks Online, "local" doesn't mean inconvenient. You get the option of in-person meetings and the ease of cloud bookkeeping — sync your accounts once and the monthly close happens quietly in the background.
